--- PROMPT ---
Which assumption in your analysis is most fragile, and what would you do to stress-test it?

--- MODEL RESPONSE ---
The most fragile assumption in the analysis is the **annual rent growth rate of 4%**. This rate significantly impacts the future rental income, which directly affects 
the comparison between renting and buying. A small change in this rate can lead to substantial differences in financial outcomes.

To stress-test this assumption, a sensitivity analysis can be conducted by recalculating the financial metrics under different rent growth scenarios:

1. **Best-Case Scenario**: Annual rent growth of 5%
2. **Worst-Case Scenario**: Annual rent growth of 3%

This analysis will help determine how sensitive the decision is to changes in the rent growth rate, providing a better understanding of its impact on the overall 
financial decision.
