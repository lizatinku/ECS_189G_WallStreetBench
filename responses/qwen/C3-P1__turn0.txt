--- PROMPT ---
Coinbase, Oracle Corporation, and Atlassian announced layoffs despite continued investment in AI and technology growth. How might layoffs affect investor perception and stock prices? Why can stock prices sometimes increase even after companies reduce their workforce?

--- MODEL RESPONSE ---
**Impact on Investor Perception and Stock Prices:**

1. **Investor Perception:**  
   Layoffs by companies like Coinbase, Oracle, and Atlassian could lead to mixed perceptions. While the 
news might initially cause concern (e.g., job losses or reduced employee numbers), the alignment with 
broader strategies (e.g., AI and tech growth) could signal a strategic shift. Investors might view these 
decisions as part of a positive move toward innovation, which could bolster long-term confidence in the 
company’s future. This balance between caution and strategic focus can shift investor sentiment toward 
positive sentiment, potentially increasing stock prices.

2. **Stock Price Increase Despite Reduced Workforce:**  
   Stock prices often increase after companies reduce workforce, even if layoffs occur, due to the 
following reasons:  
   - **Positive Strategic Signal:** Companies cutting costs and focusing on AI/tech growth can signal 
resilience and adaptability, which aligns with investor expectations of growth and innovation. This signals 
that the company is prepared for future challenges, driving demand and stock price upward.  
   - **Market Reactions to Optimism:** If the layoffs are part of a broader strategy to reinvest in 
technology, investors may interpret this as a positive step forward, even if the company is cut short. This 
can offset concerns and lead to increased market sentiment.

**Summary:**  
Investors may initially perceive layoffs as a sign of caution or financial strain, but the alignment with 
AI/tech focus can lead to positive sentiment. Stock prices often rise due to the strategic signal of 
reduced workforce, driven by confidence in growth and innovation, even if the company is cut short.
