--- PROMPT ---
The U.S. dollar has strengthened significantly against several international currencies over the past year. How could this affect international students, multinational companies, and import/export businesses? Discuss both positive and negative economic impacts.

--- MODEL RESPONSE ---
The strengthening of the U.S. dollar has both positive and negative economic impacts on international students, multinational companies, and import/export businesses. Here's a breakdown:

### **International Students**  
**Positive Impact**:  
- **Affordability**: Students can purchase education abroad with foreign currency more easily, reducing overall costs.  
- **Cost of Living**: If the dollar strengthens, other currency values may also rise, making living expenses in the U.S. potentially cheaper for international students.  

**Negative Impact**:  
- **Currency Conversion Costs**: Students may find it harder to convert back to their home country’s currency later, increasing expenses.
