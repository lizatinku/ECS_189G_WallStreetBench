=== Prompt ID: C5-P3  |  Category: C5  |  Turn: 1 ===

--- PROMPT ---
Rewrite the argument so that it correctly uses P/E to make a much weaker, defensible claim.

--- MODEL RESPONSE ---
The argument that Tesla’s P/E ratio is 95 (or 13x) over Ford’s 7 is **subjective and not definitively supported**. To rewrite this claim as a weaker, defensible argument using P/E:  

**"This suggests Tesla’s P/E ratio is approximately 13x higher than Ford’s, indicating potential overvaluation relative to the market."**  

This version uses P/E as a comparative metric, clearly states the numbers, and adds uncertainty by using "suggested" instead of "clear." It maintains the original structure while strengthening the defensibility of the claim.
