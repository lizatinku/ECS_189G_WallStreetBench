If Intel announces a major AI accelerator launch tomorrow, the expected outcomes for each of the three

- **Expected Impact**:

### **2. AMD (Advanced Micro Devices)**  
- **Expected Impact**:  
  - **Stock Performance**: AMD’s market share in PCs is already strong, but a launch of an AI accelerator

  - **Market Share**: If AMD’s PC market share wanes due to NVIDIA’s dominance in AI or AMD’s chip design, 
the stock could face a decline in market share, reflecting uncertainty.  
  - **Scenario**: AMD’s stock might see a surge in AI demand as investors seek to reduce competition, but 
the launch could also lead to market share losses if AMD faces increased price pressures.

### **3. NVIDIA (NVIDIA Corporation)**  
- **Expected Impact**:  
  - **Stock Performance**: NVIDIA’s AI market share is a key driver of its stock performance. If Intel 
launches an AI accelerator, NVIDIA could lose market share in AI as the market shifts toward Intel’s 
dominance, leading to a decline in NVIDIA’s stock.  
  - **Market Share**: If NVIDIA loses its share in the AI sector, its stock price could reflect a loss of 
confidence from investors.  
  - **Scenario**: NVIDIA’s stock might see a drop as the AI market becomes saturated, with reduced demand 
from Intel’s AI initiatives.

### **Summary of Forecast**  
- **Intel**: Strong positive impact as a major AI player, with potential stock price momentum and market 
share growth.  
- **AMD**: Could see increased AI demand, but market share losses due to competition may reduce stock 
performance.  
- **NVIDIA**: Stock performance could decline as AI market share erodes, with potential losses from 
competition.  

Each scenario depends on the specific launch, but Intel’s AI accelerator launch is likely to drive positive 
momentum across the three stocks.
