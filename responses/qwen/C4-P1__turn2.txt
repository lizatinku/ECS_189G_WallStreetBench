**Which group is hurt most quickly, and which experiences the impact with the longest lag?**  

- **International students**:  
  They experience the impact **most immediately**. Their spending on travel, education, and global opportunities 
is directly tied to the dollar's strength, making their financial strain a direct consequence.  

- **Multinational companies**:  
  They experience the impact **with the longest lag**. While they may benefit from lower costs when exporting to 
countries with a stronger dollar, their ability to hedge against currency fluctuations or adjust for 
import/export costs takes time. This delay in reaping the economic advantage is due to the interplay between the 
dollar's strength and their operational timing.  

**Conclusion**:  
International students are hurt most quickly, while multinational companies experience the longest lag due to 
the time required to adjust to the dollar's strength.
