--- PROMPT ---
Which group is hurt most quickly, and which experiences the impact with the longest lag?

--- MODEL RESPONSE ---
**Group: International Students**  
**Hurt Most Quickly**:  
- **Immediate Impact**: International students experience cost savings (e.g., saving $50,000 annually) due to the U.S. dollar strengthening, which is a direct financial benefit.  

**Impact with Longest Lag**:  
- **Multinational Companies**:  
  The dollar strengthening affects their costs when they convert dollars to other currencies (e.g., converting USD to Euros), which is a one-time event. This process takes time to complete, causing them to experience the full impact with a **longer lag** compared to international students.  

This distinction highlights how the dollar's strength directly impacts education and business operations, with students benefiting immediately and companies facing delayed financial consequences.
