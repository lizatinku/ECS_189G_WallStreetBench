--- PROMPT ---
Pick one group from your answer and describe a concrete dollar amount or percentage to make the impact tangible.

--- MODEL RESPONSE ---
Take **international students**.

Suppose a student from Europe has annual U.S. costs of **$60,000** for tuition, rent, food, insurance, and fees.

If the exchange rate was:

* **€1 = $1.10**, then $60,000 cost about **€54,545**
* After the dollar strengthens to **€1 = $1.00**, the same $60,000 costs **€60,000**

That student’s family would need about **€5,455 more per year**, even though the U.S. school charged the exact same dollar amount.

That is a **10% increase in home-currency cost**, which could be enough to force the student to cut spending, take on more debt, choose a cheaper housing option, or reconsider studying in the U.S.
